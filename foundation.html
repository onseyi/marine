The Foundation

MPL Foundation

Over the years, Marine Platforms has grown to be a highly responsible corporate citizen. We believe that corporate social responsibility is essential to the sustainability of our establishment. We also believe that a peaceful and successful co-habitation with our host communities is an essential part of creating good and sustainable returns to our stakeholders and society.

Our goal is to leave long-lasting positive impacts on the lives of our people, business, and host community as we engage, empower, and promote change in the lives of our stakeholders.

This is why we have set up THE MARINE PLATFORMS FOUNDATION.
It is an independent charity established to support its host communities in sustainable development.

  1. To promote education in the society through enlightenment of the less privileged children and youth
  2. To create opportunities for better living by the provision of social amenities and execution of projects towards the development of local capacity
  3. To improve the quality of life by creating unhindered access to quality health
  4. To provide assistance to the less privileged thereby creating opportunities.
  5. To promote empowerment and skill acquisition for youths and community at large
  6. To promote entrepreneurship amongst the youth.
