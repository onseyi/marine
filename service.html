Brown Field Development

As we continue our entry through new frontiers, we recognize the offerings deeply rooted in our ever dynamic and challenging offshore environments. This is why we have stepped to provide an extended range of brownfield development services to our customers. Our Engineering, Procurement, Installation and Commissioning division is fully catered to deliver with top-notch expertise. This rapid development in capacity has empowered us in servicing the complex demands in the following areas.

SURF (Subsea, Umbilicals, Risers & Flowlines)
Marine Platforms brings a hands-on approach into the SURF market. As we believe in exceeding our client’s expectation, our culture promotes highly dedicated project teams to all onshore and offshore subsea operations in order to increase effectiveness and mitigate risks. We strive to ensure the best service is delivered on optimal projects schedules. Today we have a growing track record in SURF activities with major oil & gas operators in Nigeria. In 2014, Marine Platforms delivered her first service of this kind on NAE Abo-8 well completion: load-out, transportation and installation of company provided items. Abo 3 and Abo 12 flowline replacement- flowline & riser transport & installation followed after. In 2016, MPL was awarded the transportation, installation and precommissioning of flexible flowlines, jumpers, umbilicals and manifolds on Chevron’s Agbami deepwater field Nigeria.

Project Management & Engineering
In Marine platforms we believe that the safe delivery of successful projects depends solely on thorough project management and engineering proficiency. Our project management team have extensive experience and expertise in planning, engineering and design, offshore construction, offshore installation, commissioning as well as decommissioning. As we continue to proactively develop this capability, we aim significantly add value to every phase of our projects.

Subsea Solutions

Marine Platforms is a leading subsea solutions provider fully equipped to safely deliver complex deep-water projects. Operating with state of the art equipment manned by highly skilled and dedicated people, our deep-water operations are ever expanding as a result of quality services delivered safely to the industry over the years.

Today’s new generation deep water assets require tremendous investment and the success of the assets operation can rely on our extensive services and support. We believe that top quality equipment, seasoned and properly trained personnel, proactive planning, rigorous safety and risk assessment procedures are key components of a successful subsea operation. With several years of experience in providing ROV services operators in Nigeria and west Africa, Marine Platforms can provide a highly reliable degree of solutions for drilling support operations.

Life of Field
We deliver safe, modern and professional Inspection Repair and Maintenance (IRM) solutions for Life of Field (LOF) services with the use of equipment which in most times are custom built to meet the uniqueness of our operations. Our IRM offering brings robust solutions on the integrity management of various subsea assets. Our preserve for cost effective quality delivery differentiates us from other subsea service providers and has also made us a preferred company. By combining a vast resource of expertise with cutting edge technological innovations, we are a company that ensures projects are always delivered timely within budget.

Drill Support
As offshore operations move into waters beyond 10,000feet, Remotely Operated Vehicles (ROV), procedures and services are highly critical to successful drilling operation. Marine Platforms state of the art, high capacity WROVs handled by seasoned ROV personnel is the ideal fit for today’s drilling operational demands. Supporting drilling, completions, exploration and production by providing advanced observation, data transmission, survey site, acoustic beacon placement & recovery, emergency BOP release, recovery of dropped objects, wellhead setting and a host of others are only a few in the wide spectrum of our drill support services.

  • Remotely operated vehicles (ROVS) Intervention.
  • Specialized ROV tooling
  • Inspection, repair and maintenance
  • Project management and logistics support
  • Subsea construction and intervention
  • Survey and positioning
  • Subsea Integrity management

Well Services

Marine Platforms started its well services operation partnering with Well-Flow International for the provision of wellbore cleanout tools and chemicals. This is a process of running mechanical tools and pumping cleanout chemicals to remove unwanted debris as well as metal shavings from the wellbore and displace the well to a clean filtered completion fluid prior to running completion strings.

We have successfully carried out this operation over the years for SNEPCo on the Bonga field, Exxon-Mobil on the Unam, Ubit, Usari, Mfem and Ehra field, Total on the Akpo field, NAE on the Abo field and Afren on the Ebok, Okoro and Okwok field.

We are currently breaking new frontiers through the deployment of coiled tubing on our vessel for acid injection operation. In strategic alliance with other technical partners we offer other services such as:

  • Well intervention
  • Well stimulation
  • Temporary plug and abandonment
  • Well suspension
  • Wellbore clean-up tooling & services
  • Wireline services
  • Slickline services
  • Tophole and rigless drilling
